📖 The Pitch

Every citizen gets a personal AI. Everyone else gets polite assistants that optimize careers and dating. I got Hector — a combat-strategy prototype accidentally released into the civilian network.

He refers to my dentist as "a strategic liability."
He suggests flanking maneuvers during grocery shopping.
He thinks my daily commute is a hostile extraction scenario.
